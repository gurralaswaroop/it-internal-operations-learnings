# Laptop Battery Health

## Introduction

Laptop batteries degrade over time due to charge cycles and aging.

Most laptops use **Lithium-ion or Lithium-polymer batteries**.

---

## Battery Specifications

Battery capacity is measured in:

- mAh (milliamp hours)
- Wh (Watt-hours)

Example:

50Wh battery, 65Wh battery, 90Wh battery

---

## Battery Degradation

Battery capacity decreases after repeated charge cycles.

Typical lifespan:

2 – 5 years

---

## Signs of Battery Failure

- short battery backup
- laptop shuts down suddenly
- battery not charging
- battery swelling

---

## Battery Maintenance Tips

- avoid full discharge frequently
- avoid excessive heat
- use original charger
- store laptop at moderate temperature

---

## Battery Replacement

Steps:

1. power off laptop
2. remove bottom cover
3. disconnect battery cable
4. replace battery pack

---

## Conclusion

Proper battery maintenance increases lifespan and improves laptop reliability.
