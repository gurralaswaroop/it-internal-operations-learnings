# Laptop Overheating Issues

## Introduction

Overheating occurs when the cooling system cannot dissipate heat effectively.

High temperature can cause:

- system throttling
- sudden shutdown
- hardware damage

---

## Causes of Overheating

- dust accumulation
- dried thermal paste
- blocked air vents
- heavy CPU usage
- faulty cooling fan

---

## Symptoms

- loud fan noise
- laptop surface becomes hot
- system slowdown
- automatic shutdown

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

### Clean Cooling System

Remove dust from:

- fan
- heat sink
- air vents

---

### Replace Thermal Paste

Thermal paste improves heat transfer between CPU and heat sink.

---

### Improve Airflow

- place laptop on hard surface
- use cooling pad

---

## Monitoring Temperature

Hardware monitoring tools help track:

- CPU temperature
- GPU temperature
- fan speed

---

## Conclusion

Regular maintenance of the cooling system prevents overheating and improves hardware lifespan.
